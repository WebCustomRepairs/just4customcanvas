A Family Journey

It began as a dream to start a new life in Santa Barbara, California, and became a reality ten years ago when our patriarch and founder, Luis Ramirez, established Just4u Custom Canvas.

In the beginning, it was a new place, a new life, and a new business, but Luis had twenty years of experience in the Textile industry in Mexico City. That set a solid foundation for us as a company. Luis brought his vision and finely honed skills with him as we set about making his dream come true. It became a dream we share and have fulfilled together and continue to build upon as a family.

Now, with over ten years of experience working in the Santa Barbara Area for many outstanding local commercial properties and residences, we have built an outstanding reputation as a business that delivers on its promises. Our guiding principle is the motto, From our family to your home or business, we assure you that youll always receive courteous service and unmatched quality.

We Deliver Excellence

Just 4u Custom Canvas delivers on our promises. We put thoughtful care and precision into all aspects of our process with results that are unsurpassed in beauty, style, durability, and strength.

Luis and our family handle every aspect of each project from beginning to end. This one-on-one interaction ensures that the custom project we create just for you will be professionally designed, installed, and properly made!

Just 4U Custom Canvas Inc is a licensed contractor with over 10 years of experience in the awning and shade industry. As a leading awning company, we specialize in awning fabrication, installation, and repair. We offer a wide selection of custom awnings for both residential and commercial properties. Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality products and personalized service to our customers in the Santa Barbara and greater Central Coast area.
